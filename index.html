About Iron Sentry LEMC

Iron Sentry LEMC was founded in 2025 in Texarkana, Texas. Iron Sentry is primarily composed of Correctional Officers and Law Enforcement. We are about brotherhood, integrity, loyalty, and never leaving our law enforcement brothers or sisters behind. This is a NO BS club that follows its own path.
